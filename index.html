Partner at Deloitte. Dr. Sebastian Müller-Bellé advises clients across Deloitte Digital's extensive portfolio with the aim of helping clients create lasting value based on superior customer experiences and driving loyalty and profitable growth. Its focus is on clients in the life sciences, pharma, medtech and healthcare sectors, particularly healthcare providers. The planning and management of global transformation projects with the design of business processes and the introduction of new cloud-based technologies in the areas of sales, service and marketing are among his main areas of consulting. Sebastian Müller-Bellé has been working in consulting at Deloitte since 2005.
